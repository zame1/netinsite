NET IN SITE

Make your awesome business idea a reality with innovation.

Net in sites are a web design company

based in Auckland, New Zealand. We primarily serve small to medium size companies and have a practical, commonsense view of how the Internet can help you. We can be flashy if you like but we prefer to build Websites that improve your business.

our services

consulting

At NetInsites we know that using the Internet effectively is more than using a Designer to put together a Website. Your Internet strategy must be integrated within your business planning process and should ensure that you communicate more successfully with all your stakeholders.

E-commerce

Your e-commerce site will be based on OpenCart an open source e-commerce system. We have modified and improved the OpenCart system. You will need to choose one of the available OpenCart templates to set the structure of the site. The idea is to make the selling of your products the focus of the site.

web development

We design and build highly effective Website solutions for our clients. Before we build a Website we look closely at the strategy behind the proposal and make sure it fits with the proposed design and structure. Although we use the latest Internet technology and keep up with the latest trends the effectiveness of the Website always comes first.
